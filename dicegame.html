Dice Game!


First player to score the most points within three rounds win! wins!

Round One: Roll the 20 and the 12 sided dice.

Round Two: Roll the 10 and the 8 sided dice.

Final Round: Roll the 6 and the 4 sided die.

Roll a "one" on Both dice during any of the three rounds and it's an automatic WIN!

Roll a "five" on Both dice during any of the three rounds and you can STEAL the other players points!
